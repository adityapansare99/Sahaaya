# Sahaaya — NGO Compliance & Guidelines

## Registration requirements
NGOs must provide a valid registration number, contact person details, and proof of operation. All NGOs are verified before being approved on the platform.

## Daily capacity
NGOs must specify their daily capacity for receiving and distributing food. This helps match donations to the right organizations.

## Distribution requirements
NGOs must distribute received food to people in need within a reasonable timeframe. Food should not be wasted or resold. NGOs are expected to maintain hygiene standards at their distribution points.

## Record keeping
NGOs should maintain records of all food received and distributed. The platform tracks donation history automatically. These records can be accessed through the NGO dashboard.

## Compliance with local laws
NGOs must comply with all applicable local, state, and national laws regarding food handling and distribution. This includes obtaining necessary licenses and permits.

## Reporting requirements
If any issue arises with a donation, NGOs should report it through the platform immediately. This helps maintain quality standards for all participants.
